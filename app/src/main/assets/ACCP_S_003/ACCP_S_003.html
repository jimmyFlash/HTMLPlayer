Management

Stepwise approach to control symptoms and minimize future risk

ICS= Inhaled glucocorticosteroids. LABA: long acting beta 2 agonist. Med: medium dose. OCS: oral corticosteroid. Anti IgE: anti-immunoglobulin E therapy.
* For children 6-11 years; Theophylline is not recommended, and the preferred step 3 treatment is medium dose ICS.
**Low dose ICS/formoterol is the reliever medication needed for patients prescribed low dose budesonide /formeterol or low dose beclometasone/ formoterol maintenance and reliever therapy.
